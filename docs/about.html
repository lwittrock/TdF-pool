About the Pool

How It Works

Welcome to the 2025 Tour de France Fantasy Pool! This is a simple "set it and forget it" pool where each participant picks a team of riders before the Grand DÃ©part. Points are automatically calculated based on daily stage results, General Classification (GC) standings, and special jersey holders throughout the Tour.

The leaderboard updates daily, usually after each stage concludes, giving you a live overview of how your team is performing.

Scoring Rules

  • **Stage Winner (1st Place):** 25 points
  • **Stage Top 20:** Points decrease from 24 (2nd place) down to 5 (20th place).
  • **Yellow Jersey (GC Leader):** 10 points per day.
  • **Green Jersey (Points Classification Leader):** 5 points per day.
  • **Polka Dot Jersey (Mountains Classification Leader):** 5 points per day.
  • **White Jersey (Best Young Rider Classification Leader):** 5 points per day.

Only the specific rider holding the jersey on that day earns points. If a rider holds multiple jerseys, they earn points for each one.

Participation

This is a private pool among friends/colleagues. If you have questions about joining next year, please contact the pool administrator.
